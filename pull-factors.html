Tips when moving with a family

Pull Factors

When I began my search, the jobs advertised for a classroom teacher position on the TES site, with no additional management responsibilities, would give me a net salary of around 30% more than my take-home, as an Upper Pay Scale teacher in England. On top of this, were end of salary bonuses worth around 4000GBP per year and accommodation allowances too. We would even be able to afford to come home and visit family each summer, as many jobs were providing an annual flight allowance for the employees. These perks were fairly typical of the jobs offered in schools in China, Malaysia, Thailand, etc. Some schools even offer to pay for medical insurance and offer a 500GBP payment at the start of contract to buy homeware items. As my husband is also a teacher, we could double most of these benefits.
