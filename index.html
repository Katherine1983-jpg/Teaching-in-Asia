Teaching in Asia ✈

Tips when moving with a family

The allure of a dramatically improved salary, the opportunity to travel, an upgraded classroom environment, free education for your dependents and friendly, dedicated students cannot be overstated when it comes to making the decision to submit an application to a school in Asia. I’m fortunate enough to have experienced a myriad of these advantages in the years I’ve spent teaching in Asia. I’ve also run into my fair share of challenges. This website is here to provide the hints and tips you’ll need to make the decision that’s right for you before you leave it all behind and step on the plane!
